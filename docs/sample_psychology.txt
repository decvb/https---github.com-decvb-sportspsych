Mental Toughness in Sports

Mental toughness is a key psychological trait for athletes. It involves resilience, focus, and the ability to perform under pressure. Techniques to improve mental toughness include visualization, goal setting, and positive self-talk.

Visualization: Athletes imagine themselves succeeding in their sport, which can improve confidence and performance.

Goal Setting: Setting specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals helps athletes stay motivated and track progress.

Positive Self-Talk: Encouraging internal dialogue can help athletes overcome setbacks and maintain motivation.
